<p>
DataDocks provides a live, interactive map for your yard operations. Stop relying on radios and clipboards. Get complete visibility of trailer locations and empower your team to schedule moves and track dwell times alongside <a href="/datadocks-features/dock-dashboard">dock scheduling</a>. For a broader look at how yard management fits into warehouse operations, see our <a href="/yard-management">yard management system</a> overview.
</p>

<h3>
Core Capabilities
</h3>

<ul>

<li>
<strong>Interactive Yard Map:</strong> Drag and drop trailers to different parking spots or dock doors.
</li>

<li>
<strong>Color-Coded Dwell Timers:</strong> Spot aging inventory instantly with clear visual indicators.
</li>

<li>
<strong>Digital Move Orders:</strong> Dispatch tasks directly to yard jockey mobile devices with priority queuing.
</li>

<li>
<strong>Unified Scheduling:</strong> View yard operations and dock scheduling side by side.
</li>

</ul>

<h3>
Multi-Site and Cross-Facility
</h3>

<p>
Each facility gets its own dock door layout, yard zones, capacity rules, and custom fields. Your corporate team gets standardized reporting across every site without forcing identical processes on the floor.
</p>

<h3>
Connects to Your Existing Stack
</h3>

<p>
DataDocks sits between your TMS and your WMS. REST API, webhooks, EDI-via-API bridge, and SSO (OAuth 2.0 / SAML) come out of the box. Production integrations with SAP S/4HANA, Oracle Fusion Cloud, NetSuite, and Microsoft Power BI. See the full list on the <a href="/integrations">integrations page</a>.
</p>
