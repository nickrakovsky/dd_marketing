    answer: "Yes — they're the same system. An appointment booked in the dock dashboard carries through to the yard as the trailer arrives, stages, and gets assigned to a door.     answer: "DataDocks tracks trailer dwell time, turnaround time by carrier, gate-to-dock time, yard-spot utilization, and late or missed appointments. Use these to spot carriers causing detention, identify bottlenecks between yard and dock, and benchmark facility performance month over month."
