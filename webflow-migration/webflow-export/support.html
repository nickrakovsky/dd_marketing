Do customers/carrier accounts count towards User Accounts?

You will get unlimited customer/carrier/vendor accounts

Can my customers/carriers see all my appointments?

No. The only appointments they can see are the appointments tied to their account.

I have many different locations

DataDocks was designed to scale horizontally, this is not a problem for our software!

I want to run this as a standalone product

Perfect! DataDocks works great this way.

DataDocks can integrate with a lot of different systems. We also have an API that's accessible. Contact us to see how we can help.

There's no minimum contract for DataDocks
